Has your SaaS growth hit a plateau?

Many bootstrapped SaaS founders hit a growth ceiling between $10k and $30k in MRR. At this stage, you’ve built something great, but scaling further can feel like an uphill battle. You’re faced with a tough decision:

  • Hire others to help scale, eating into your profits.
  • Keep brute-forcing growth, risking burnout and stagnation.
  • Do nothing, leaving your business vulnerable to competitors and market shifts.
